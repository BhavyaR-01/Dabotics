Caesar Salad

A Caesar Salad is a timeless and easy-to-make dish that's both delicious and refreshing. To prepare it, start by washing and chopping a head of fresh romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces. Next, in a large salad bowl, add the lettuce and some croutons for that satisfying crunch. For the creamy Caesar dressing, mix together 1/2 cup of mayonnaise, 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese, 2 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice, 1 teaspoon of Dijon mustard, 2 minced garlic cloves, and a pinch of salt and black pepper. Drizzle the dressing over the lettuce and croutons, then toss everything together until the lettuce is coated evenly. To finish, top the salad with additional grated Parmesan cheese and garnish with anchovy fillets (optional). Serve it as a side dish or add grilled chicken or shrimp for a complete meal. This classic Caesar Salad is a crowd-pleaser and a perfect addition to any meal. Enjoy!
