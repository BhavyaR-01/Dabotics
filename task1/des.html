Delicious Recipes

Recipe Name 1:chocolate cake

Mix together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and baking soda.
Add eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract; mix until smooth.
Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 30-35 minutes; let cool, then enjoy your chocolate cake!

Recipe Name 2:ice cream

Combine heavy cream, condensed milk, and vanilla extract in a bowl.
Whip the mixture until it's thick and creamy. Freeze for at least 4 hours, then serve your homemade vanilla ice cream!
